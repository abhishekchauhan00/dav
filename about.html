Mr. Samundra Sain

Mr. Samundra sain is regarded as one of the pioneering forces in teaching and learning in contemporary India. In recent decades, academicians and educationists have been able to bring a striking shift from inaccessible and archaic academic setups in Indian schools and colleges. Mr. Samundra commitment to qualitatively change the previous scenario into a dynamic and systematic learning environment has, in fact, provided the impetus for this shift in Uttar Pradesh and beyond. Since 2008, Mr. Samundra Sain has worked towards a seamless integration of learning made accessible and knowledge made attainable. His ideology and vision are to ensure that every student is provided with the foundation, the capabilities and the means to achieve rigour , skill and, ultimately, success.

The D.A.V. is committed to instill well-rounded academic rigor and imbue the zeal for a competition so that one’s course of action can lead them to reach the zenith of success meaningfully.
We are in an institution who believe in constantly attempting to achieve both short-term objectives and long-term goals in balanced measure. The D.A.V. strongly emphasizes the true nature of determination in pursuit of the most tangible forms of academic success.
